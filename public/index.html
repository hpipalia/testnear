HIV & STI Resources

Evidence-based information from CDC, NIH, and HRSA.

🔬
What Is HIV Testing?
HIV tests check blood or oral fluid for the virus. Rapid tests give results in 20 minutes. Regular testing is essential if sexually active.
CDC HIV Testing →
💊
What Is PrEP?
PrEP reduces HIV risk by over 99% when taken daily. Free through ADAP, Medicaid, and manufacturer assistance programs.
Learn About PrEP →
🛡
How Often Should I Test?
CDC recommends annual HIV testing if sexually active. Every 3–6 months with multiple partners or injectable drug use.
CDC Recommendations →
🔒
Confidential vs. Anonymous
Confidential: Linked to ID but legally protected. Anonymous: No personal info — code only. Many sites offer both options.
What to Expect →
🧾
STI Testing Explained
Many STIs have no symptoms. Chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis, hepatitis — all detected with simple tests. Treatment is highly effective when caught early.
STI Testing Guide →
🆘
Just Tested Positive?
With ART treatment, people with HIV live long, healthy lives. Ryan White Program provides free HIV care nationwide. Connect with a provider now.
Starting HIV Care →
